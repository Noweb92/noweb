> 6 creatives ready to ship · Built for cold Perth audiences · Optimised for $990 conversion at $400/day budget

## 1. Strategy in 60 seconds

You're running cold traffic to a $990 purchase, no call. That's a hard sell. Three psychological doors:

1. **Problem-aware** — they know their site is bad, but procrastinate. We make staying still painful.
2. **Price-hammer** — they don't know they need a new site, but $990 (was $2000) is too good to ignore. Urgency-driven.
3. **Social-proof** — they're sceptical. We show another Perth business who took the leap and won.

Each angle gets two formats: **1 static image** (Stories, Feed) + **1 video** (Reels, Feed video). 6 total.

**Run all 6 simultaneously for the first 7 days.** Meta picks the winner. Cut the bottom 3 by ROAS at Day 7. Double down on the top 3 from Day 8.

## 4. Audience targeting (Meta Ads Manager)

### Cold prospecting set — 70% of budget ($280/day at $400 total)

**Location:** Perth, WA + 30km radius
**Age:** 28-58
**Gender:** All
**Languages:** English

**Detailed targeting (use OR, broad):**
- Small business owner
- Restaurant management
- Square POS · Toast POS · Mindbody · Calendly (proxy for owner-operators)
- Ecommerce platform · Shopify · Squarespace
- Behaviours: "Small business owners"
- Interests: "Entrepreneurship", "Marketing"

**Exclude:**
- Web designers, web developers, freelancers (waste of budget)
- People who already work at agencies

**Placement:** Auto. But manually exclude Audience Network — too low quality.

### Retargeting set — 20% of budget ($80/day)

**Audience 1:** Website visitors last 14 days, didn't purchase
**Audience 2:** Brief form viewers, didn't submit
**Audience 3:** Watched 50%+ of any video creative, didn't visit landing

**Run different creatives to retargeting** — testimonial style works best for these warm audiences.

### Lookalike set — 10% of budget ($40/day)

Don't activate Day 1. Activate from Day 14, after you have 30+ Stripe checkouts logged in pixel.

**Source:** Lookalike 1% Australia from your purchaser pixel data.

## 5. Pixel events to track

Set up these standard events in Meta Pixel:

| Event | When it fires | Why |
|---|---|---|
| `PageView` | Landing page load | Baseline |
| `ViewContent` | User scrolls 50%+ on landing | Engagement signal |
| `InitiateCheckout` | Click on "Claim your spot" CTA | Pre-purchase signal, optimise on this for first 14 days |
| `Lead` | Brief form submitted (post-payment) | High intent, Care upsell potential |
| `Purchase` | Stripe payment success → success URL on landing | The big one |

**For first 14 days, optimise campaign on `InitiateCheckout`** — you don't have enough Purchase data yet for the algo to learn.
**From Day 15, switch to `Purchase` optimisation** — once you have 30-50 events.

## 6. 14-day test plan

| Day | Action | Decision rule |
|---|---|---|
| **Day 0** | Launch all 6 creatives. Budget $50/day spread across 6. Optimise on InitiateCheckout. | — |
| **Day 3** | Check spend. CPM > 30 AUD or CTR < 0.8% across all creatives = pause that creative. | Cut losers fast |
| **Day 5** | Identify top 3 creatives by ROAS (or by InitiateCheckout cost if no purchases yet). | — |
| **Day 7** | Cut bottom 3 creatives. Increase budget on top 3 to $100/day each = $300/day. | Concentrate budget |
| **Day 8-10** | Activate retargeting set ($80/day) with testimonial creative #5 or #6. | Reach the warm audience |
| **Day 11** | First scaling decision. If ROAS > 2x, push top creative to $200/day. | — |
| **Day 14** | Switch optimisation event from InitiateCheckout to Purchase. Activate Lookalike set. | Mature funnel |
| **Day 21** | Refresh creatives — fatigue kicks in by Day 18-21 in a market the size of Perth. Make 3 new variants of the top angle. | Combat fatigue |

**Kill criteria for any creative:**
- 100 AUD spent, 0 InitiateCheckout → kill
- CTR < 0.5% on Day 2 → kill
- ROAS < 1.5x by Day 7 → kill

## 8. What to NOT do

- **Don't put a phone number in the ad.** No call = no call. Stick to the no-call funnel.
- **Don't use stock photos of laptops on desks.** Generic AI slop. Stick to typography, real product shots, real client work.
- **Don't run 1 creative.** Always 6 minimum at launch. Meta needs variety to learn.
- **Don't change creatives daily.** Let each one accumulate $100 minimum spend before judging.
- **Don't optimise for "Reach" or "Brand Awareness."** You're selling, optimise for action.
- **Don't run during low-intent times.** Schedule ads 6am-11pm Perth time. Pause overnight if budget tight.
- **Don't ignore Reels.** 60%+ of your young owner-operator audience scrolls Reels. Make sure your videos play vertical.
