The story.

Saltwood began in a converted boatshed in 2019, with a wood-fired oven, six tables, and a stubborn belief that breakfast should never be rushed. We bake our sourdough at four in the morning. We pull our coffee from beans roasted nine kilometres away. And we plate every dish like it matters.

Five years later, the boatshed is gone but the philosophy isn't. Our kitchen runs on long mornings, honest produce, and the kind of slow craft that the coast quietly demands.
